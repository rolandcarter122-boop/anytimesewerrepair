Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my yard smell like a sewer?

An unpleasant smell in your yard often means there’s a leak or break in your sewer line. It’s best to have a professional plumber inspect your sewer system to locate and fix the issue before it worsens.

What DIY method can I use to solve backups in my toilet drain?

You can try using a plunger or a drain snake for minor clogs. However, if the backup keeps returning or affects multiple drains, it’s likely a deeper sewer issue that requires professional cleaning or hydrojetting.

Do you fix sewer repairs on holidays?

Yes, we do. We offer 24/7 emergency sewer repair services, including weekends and holidays.

Do you provide routine sewer maintenance?

Absolutely. We make a scheduled plan for sewer inspections and cleaning services to keep your system flowing.

Do you help clear blockages in sewer pipes?

Absolutely! We clear clogs like grease buildup, tree roots, or debris blocking your pipe using a hydrojetting machine, which also cleans the pipe internally.
