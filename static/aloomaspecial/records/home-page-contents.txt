Types of Sewers We Service<h2>
At #State Sewer Line Repair, our technicians are skilled and experienced in installing, repairing, and maintaining different types of sewer systems, including:<list>
Sanitary Sewer System: This system carries wastewater from bathrooms, sinks, and other indoor plumbing fixtures to a treatment plant or septic system where it’s properly treated and disposed of.^^
Storm Sewer System: This system handles rainwater and runoff from roofs, roads, and parking lots. It directs stormwater safely away from your property to prevent flooding or erosion.^^
Combined Sewer System: A combined system that carries both sanitary wastewater and stormwater in the same pipe. ^^
Industrial Sewer System: They are used in factories and large facilities; this system collects and channels industrial wastewater that often contains chemicals or by-products so that they are treated safely before disposal.
